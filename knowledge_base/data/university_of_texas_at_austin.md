# Information for University of Texas at Austin

## General Requirements
Applicants need a bachelor’s degree from an accredited institution with a minimum GPA of 3.0/4.0.
**Note on GPA:** GPA requirements are typically on a 4.0 scale. Users should convert their 10-point CGPA accordingly for comparison.

## TOEFL Requirements
The minimum TOEFL iBT score is 79, though competitive applicants often exceed 100. An IELTS score of 6.5+ is accepted.

## GRE Requirements
The GRE is optional for most programs and is required only for a few technical disciplines.

## Application Deadlines
PhD deadlines are typically December 1st. Master’s deadlines are between December 15th and January 15th.

## Required Documents
- Statement of Purpose (SOP)
- 3 Letters of Recommendation
- Transcripts
- CV / Resume

## Job Opportunities
Graduates benefit from Austin’s thriving tech ecosystem, with job placements at companies like Dell, Tesla, Oracle, and Google.
