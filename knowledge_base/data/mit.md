## TOEFL Requirements
International candidates from non-English backgrounds must submit TOEFL iBT or IELTS scores. For example, the EAPS department requires a TOEFL score of 100+ or an IELTS score of 7.0+.

## GRE Requirements
Many departments expect GRE General Test scores, and some may require subject-specific GRE tests. There is no institute-wide minimum score.

## Application Deadlines
Deadlines vary by department. For instance, the MIT EAPS department has a deadline of December 1st.

## Required Documents
- Statement of Objectives / Research statement
- 3 Letters of Recommendation
- Curriculum Vitae (CV) / Resume
- Transcripts
- Departmental supplements (essays, project descriptions)

## Job Opportunities
MIT has strong industry ties and an excellent reputation in technology, research, and entrepreneurship. Graduates are well-placed in academia, research labs, tech firms, and startups.
