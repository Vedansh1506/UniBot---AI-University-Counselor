# Information for University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA)

## General Requirements
A bachelor’s degree with strong academic standing and relevant background is required.
**Note on GPA:** GPA requirements are typically on a 4.0 scale. Users should convert their 10-point CGPA accordingly for comparison.

## TOEFL Requirements
The minimum required TOEFL iBT score is 87. The minimum IELTS score is 7.0.

## GRE Requirements
The GRE is optional for most graduate programs.

## Application Deadlines
PhD deadlines are typically December 1st. Master’s deadlines are between December 15th and January 15th.

## Required Documents
- Statement of Purpose (SOP)
- 3 Letters of Recommendation
- Transcripts
- CV / Resume

## Job Opportunities
Located near Silicon Beach, UCLA students gain access to opportunities in AI, data analytics, and entertainment tech startups.
