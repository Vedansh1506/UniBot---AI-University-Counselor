# Information for Stanford University

## General Requirements
Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree (U.S. or equivalent) from a recognized institution. Each graduate program may have additional prerequisites.
**Note on GPA:** GPA requirements are typically on a 4.0 scale. Users should convert their 10-point CGPA accordingly for comparison.

## TOEFL Requirements
If your native language is not English, you may need to submit TOEFL or IELTS scores. The Graduate School of Education requires a minimum TOEFL of 90, but a score of 109 or an IELTS of 7.5 is needed to be exempt from Stanford's English placement test.

## GRE Requirements
Stanford does not have a universal minimum GRE requirement. However, certain programs (like Statistics, Economics) do require it. Strong scores improve competitiveness.

## Application Deadlines
Deadlines vary. Many PhD programs have deadlines around early December. Master’s programs may have deadlines from mid-December to mid-January.

## Required Documents
- Statement of Purpose (SOP)
- 2 or 3 Letters of Recommendation
- Curriculum Vitae (CV) / Resume
- Transcripts
- Possible supplementary materials (writing samples, portfolios)

## Job Opportunities
Stanford’s location in Silicon Valley provides unparalleled access to top tech companies and startups. The Career Education office supports students, and graduates are highly sought after in industry and academia.
