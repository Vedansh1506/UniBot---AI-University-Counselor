# Information for University of Michigan, Ann Arbor

## General Requirements
A bachelor’s degree or equivalent in a relevant field is required. A GPA of 3.0/4.0 or higher is recommended.
**Note on GPA:** GPA requirements are typically on a 4.0 scale. Users should convert their 10-point CGPA accordingly for comparison.

## TOEFL Requirements
A TOEFL iBT score of around 100 is recommended, with section scores of 23+ in Listening/Reading and 21+ in Speaking/Writing. An IELTS score of 6.5+ is accepted.

## GRE Requirements
The GRE is optional for most programs but may be required for a few STEM majors.

## Application Deadlines
PhD deadlines are typically December 1st. Master’s deadlines are between December 15th and January 15th.

## Required Documents
- Statement of Purpose (SOP)
- Letters of Recommendation
- Transcripts
- CV / Resume

## Job Opportunities
The university has a strong alumni network in the automotive, data science, and consulting industries. Major employers include Ford, Amazon, and Google.
