# Information for University of Wisconsin–Madison

## General Requirements
A bachelor’s degree in a related discipline with a solid academic foundation is required.
**Note on GPA:** GPA requirements are typically on a 4.0 scale. Users should convert their 10-point CGPA accordingly.

## TOEFL Requirements
The minimum required TOEFL iBT score is 92. The minimum IELTS score is 7.0.

## GRE Requirements
The GRE is optional but may enhance scholarship consideration.

## Application Deadlines
Fall deadlines are between December 1st and January 15th.

## Required Documents
- Statement of Purpose (SOP)
- 3 Letters of Recommendation
- Transcripts
- CV / Resume

## Job Opportunities
Graduates succeed in the research, engineering, and healthcare industries. Employers include Epic Systems, GE, and national labs.
