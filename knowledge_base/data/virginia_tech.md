# Information for Virginia Tech

## General Requirements
A bachelor’s degree in a relevant field with a strong GPA and academic background is required.
**Note on GPA:** GPA requirements are typically on a 4.0 scale. Users should convert their 10-point CGPA accordingly.

## TOEFL Requirements
The minimum required TOEFL iBT score is 80, with no subsection score less than 16. The minimum IELTS score is 6.5.

## GRE Requirements
The GRE is required for most engineering programs but is optional for others.

## Application Deadlines
Fall deadlines are between December 15th and January 15th.

## Required Documents
- Statement of Purpose (SOP)
- 3 Letters of Recommendation
- Transcripts
- CV / Resume

## Job Opportunities
Graduates are recruited by top employers like Deloitte, General Dynamics, and Northrop Grumman in the areas of cybersecurity and systems engineering.# Information for Virginia Tech
