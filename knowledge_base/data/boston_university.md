## TOEFL Requirements
The minimum required TOEFL iBT score is 84, with section minimums of Reading 21, Listening 18, Speaking 23, and Writing 22. The equivalent minimum IELTS score is 7.0.

## GRE Requirements
The GRE is optional or waived for many MA/MS programs. However, it may be required for specialized programs in engineering and science.

## Application Deadlines
Priority deadlines for many programs are around January 15th for Fall admission.

## Required Documents
- Statement of Purpose (SOP)
- 2 or 3 Letters of Recommendation
- CV / Resume
- Official Transcripts
- Possible writing samples or portfolios

## Job Opportunities
Located in Boston, BU has strong connections to the technology, healthcare, and research sectors, offering excellent opportunities for internships and full-time roles.
