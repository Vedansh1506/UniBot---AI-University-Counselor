# Information for Georgia Institute of Technology (Georgia Tech)

## General Requirements
Applicants must hold a bachelor’s degree. Some departments set minimum GPAs; for example, Materials Science & Engineering (MSE) requires a 3.0/4.0 for an MS and 3.2 for a PhD.
**Note on GPA:** GPA requirements are typically on a 4.0 scale. Users should convert their 10-point CGPA accordingly for comparison.

## TOEFL Requirements
The minimum required TOEFL iBT score is 90, with a minimum of 19 in each section.

## GRE Requirements
GRE requirements vary. Some departments, like MSE, have dropped the requirement, while others, like Computer Science, may still require it.

## Application Deadlines
Deadlines differ by program, but many require applications by December – January for Fall intake.

## Required Documents
- Statement of Purpose (SOP)
- 3 Letters of Recommendation
- CV / Resume
- Transcripts

## Job Opportunities
Located in Atlanta, Georgia Tech has strong ties to industry and tech. Graduates are highly sought after for roles in R&D, startups, and technology leadership.
