# Information for Johns Hopkins University

## General Requirements
Applicants must hold a bachelor’s degree from an accredited institution. Strong academic records and research experience are valued.
**Note on GPA:** GPA requirements are typically on a 4.0 scale. Users should convert their 10-point CGPA accordingly for comparison.

## TOEFL Requirements
The minimum required TOEFL iBT score is 100, with no section score below 26. An IELTS score of 7.0+ is also accepted.

## GRE Requirements
The GRE is optional for most programs; check departmental requirements for specific exceptions.

## Application Deadlines
PhD deadlines are typically December 1st. Master’s deadlines are usually between December 15th and January 15th.

## Required Documents
- Statement of Purpose (SOP)
- 3 Letters of Recommendation
- Official Transcripts
- CV / Resume

## Job Opportunities
Graduates are highly employable in healthcare, public policy, and biomedical research, with opportunities at institutions like NIH, FDA, and top global companies.
