# Information for University of California, Berkeley

## General Requirements
Applicants must hold a bachelor’s degree from an accredited institution. A GPA of 3.0/4.0 or higher is commonly required.
**Note on GPA:** GPA requirements are typically on a 4.0 scale. Users should convert their 10-point CGPA accordingly for comparison.

## TOEFL Requirements
International applicants typically need a TOEFL iBT score of at least 80, though 100 is preferred.

## GRE Requirements
Many Berkeley graduate programs accept or require GRE scores, though some departments have made it optional.

## Application Deadlines
Deadlines vary by department, with many falling in December – January for Fall admission.

## Required Documents
- Statement of Purpose (SOP)
- 3 Letters of Recommendation
- CV / Resume
- Official Transcripts
- Possible writing samples or supplemental statements

## Job Opportunities
Being in the San Francisco Bay Area provides proximity to many tech companies and startups. Berkeley alumni have strong placement in technology, research, and finance.
