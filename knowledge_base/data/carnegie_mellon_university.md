# Information for Carnegie Mellon University (CMU)

## TOEFL Requirements
Minimum TOEFL scores vary. The Department of English requires a TOEFL iBT of 102+ (with 25+ in each subtest) or an IELTS score of 7.5+.

## GRE Requirements
The GRE is optional for some programs (like the Computer Science PhD) but required for others.

## Application Deadlines
Deadlines vary. The School of Computer Science (SCS) has final deadlines in early December.

## Required Documents
- Statement of Purpose (SOP) / Research Proposal
- 3 Letters of Recommendation
- CV / Resume
- Official Transcripts
- Possible writing samples or portfolios

## Job Opportunities
CMU has an elite reputation in computer science, robotics, and AI, leading to outstanding placements in top tech firms and research labs.
