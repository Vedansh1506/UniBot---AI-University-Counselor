# Information for Caltech (California Institute of Technology)

## General Requirements
Applicants must hold a bachelor’s degree or equivalent from a recognized institution. Departments may require specific undergraduate coursework (e.g., math, physics).
**Note on GPA:** GPA requirements are typically on a 4.0 scale. Users should convert their 10-point CGPA accordingly for comparison.

## TOEFL Requirements
Non-native English applicants typically need a TOEFL score with a minimum of 100.

## GRE Requirements
Some programs may require GRE General or subject tests; requirements vary by department.

## Application Deadlines
Many graduate program deadlines are in December – January.

## Required Documents
- Statement of Purpose / Research Plan
- 2 or 3 Letters of Recommendation
- CV / Resume
- Transcripts
- Supplemental materials (e.g., publications, writing sample)

## Job Opportunities
Caltech graduates often pursue careers in academia, high-level research labs, aerospace, tech, and government labs.
