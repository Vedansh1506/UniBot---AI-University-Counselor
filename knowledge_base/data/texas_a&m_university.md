## TOEFL Requirements
A minimum TOEFL iBT score of 80 or an IELTS score of 6.0 is required. Some departments, like the Mays Business School, may require higher scores (TOEFL 100+ or IELTS 7.0+).

## GRE Requirements
The GRE is required for many programs but may be waived based on departmental policy. Competitive applicants typically have strong quantitative scores (160+).

## Application Deadlines
For Fall intake, deadlines are typically between December 1st and January 15th. For Spring intake, deadlines are between August 1st and September 15th.

## Required Documents
- Statement of Purpose (SOP), concise and focused (1-2 pages)
- 3 Letters of Recommendation
- Resume/CV may be required
- Official Transcripts

## Job Opportunities
Texas A&M has strong industry partnerships, especially in engineering and business. Graduates secure positions at major companies like ExxonMobil, Deloitte, HP, and NASA.
