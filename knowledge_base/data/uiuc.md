# Information for University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC)

## General Requirements

**Note on GPA:** GPA requirements are typically on a 4.0 scale. Users should convert their 10-point CGPA accordingly for comparison.

## TOEFL Requirements
For full admission status, a minimum TOEFL iBT of 103 is typically required. For limited status, the minimum is 79. An IELTS score of 6.5+ is also accepted.

## GRE Requirements
The GRE is not required for many programs, including the Computer Science MS. However, some departments may still consider or require it.

## Application Deadlines
Deadlines vary by department, but many are in December or January for Fall admission.

## Required Documents
- Statement of Purpose (SOP)
- 3 Letters of Recommendation
- CV / Resume
- Transcripts
- Possible departmental supplemental requirements

## Job Opportunities
UIUC has a strong reputation in engineering and computer science. Alumni are well-placed in high-technology firms, research labs, and startups.
